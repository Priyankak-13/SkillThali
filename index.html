Our Story

About SkillThali

SkillThali is a student-focused freelancing platform designed to connect talented students with clients who need affordable and quality services. It provides a simple and secure system where clients can post projects and students can apply and communicate easily.

The platform enables students to showcase their skills — coding, graphic design, content creation — while also providing opportunities to work on real-world projects and gain practical experience.

SkillThali aims to help students build strong portfolios, create earning opportunities, and improve placement readiness by giving hands-on industry exposure.
