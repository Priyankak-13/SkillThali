Our Story

About SkillThali

SkillThali is a student-focused freelancing platform designed to connect talented students with clients who need affordable and quality services.

The platform enables students to showcase their skills — coding, graphic design, content creation — while working on real-world projects and gaining practical experience.

SkillThali helps students build strong portfolios, create earning opportunities, and improve placement readiness through hands-on industry exposure.
